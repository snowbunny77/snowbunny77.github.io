1. Introduction

LGBTQIA+ people have existed all over history. Being queer is intertwined with being human, and for as long as we've been here, so have the queers.

However, in many cultures, queer people have been shamed for being heterosexual. The stories of queer people have been blotted out so as to not stain the respected figures we know with the label of queer that some take as negative.

Being queer used to be something to hide, lest one be subjected to ridicule, scandal, and shame. It's just now that we're able to step out of the shadows, learn to read between the lines, and learn about our hidden history.

This is only the beginning of our history. We've just began the fight and we've already made good headway in making the world more accepting of us.

I hope you learn something new from this, and I highly encourage you to do your own research on this topic.
